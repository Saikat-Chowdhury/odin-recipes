MANDI

Hello folks, myself mandi;

Mandi (Arabic: مندي) is a traditional dish that originated from Hadhramaut, Yemen, consisting mainly of meat and rice with a special blend of spices, cooked in a pit underground.

You can easily find it cooked in many different styles in hundreds of Mandi restaurants across Dubai.

Ingredients

  • 500 grams Chicken , with bones
  • 2 tablespoons Extra Virgin Olive
  • 2 cups Basmati rice
  • 4 cups Water

Steps needed for making the dish:

  1. Heat a large saucepan on medium heat and add oil.
  2. In a small mixing bowl, add soft butter and Mandi spice powder and some salt, mix well and keep aside.
  3. Bake for 20 to 25 minutes or until the skin of the chicken turns to a nice golden brown colour.
  4. Serve Arabian Chicken Mandi with Raw Mango raita and Pickled onions for a delicious weeknight dinner.
